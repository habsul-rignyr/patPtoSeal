Urbit @p to Seal Script converter

Instructions: Enter a @p without "~" or "-". This app can generate a Seal Script equivalent for galaxies, stars, and planets.

Seal should be read top to bottom, left to right on screen sizes tablet greater than 576px. On mobile devices the seal is displayed top to bottom. Both are valid forms.
